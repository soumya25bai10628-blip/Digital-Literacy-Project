#SOCIAL MEDIA DO'S AND DON'TS FOR COLLEGE STUDENTS:-

DO's 
1. Keep your profiles clean and professional at all times
2. Think before you post — ask yourself "would I be okay if my professor saw this?"
3. Use strong and unique passwords for every platform
4. Report any abusive, fake or suspicious accounts or content
5. Give proper credit when sharing someone else's work or ideas
6. Use your real name and college email for professional platforms like LinkedIn and GitHub
7. Keep your privacy settings updated and reviewed regularly

DON'Ts 
1. Don't share personal details like your phone number, address or ID publicly
2. Don't post anything during exams, classes or college events
3. Don't forward or share unverified news or rumours
4. Don't accept follow or friend requests from people you don't know
5. Don't post anything negative about your college, professors or classmates
6. Don't use the same password across multiple platforms
7. Don't overshare your daily routine or location publicly
